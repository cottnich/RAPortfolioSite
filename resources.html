1. Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Website: CAPS Website

Contact Info:

  • Phone: (814) 863-0395
  • Address: 501 Student Health Center, University Park, PA 16802

CAPS offers mental health support through counseling, crisis intervention, and wellness workshops to help students handle personal and academic challenges.

2. Student Disability Resources (SDR)

Website: SDR Website

Contact Info:

  • Phone: (814) 863-1807
  • Address: 116 Boucke Building, University Park, PA 16802

Website: CSGD Website

Contact Info:

Website: Career Services Website

Contact Info:

  • Phone: (814) 865-2377
  • Address: 103 Bank of America Career Services Center, University Park, PA 16802

Career Services helps with job searches, resume reviews, mock interviews, and networking opportunities.

5. Lion's Pantry

Website: Lion's Pantry Website

Contact Info:

  • Phone: 814-865-4700
